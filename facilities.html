Central Analytical Core Facilities

The Hawaii Institute of Materials Research (HIMaR) oversees a centralized core of advanced analytical facilities supporting materials research across disciplines. These shared resources are designed to enable innovation by providing researchers with access to powerful tools for structural, chemical, and morphological characterization of materials.

Our analytical core serves academic researchers, students, industry partners, and government collaborators. With expertise across a wide range of measurement techniques and advanced instrumentation, HIMaR supports research in sustainability, energy, environment, and advanced manufacturing.

Available Techniques

  • X-ray Diffraction (XRD)
  • X-ray Fluorescence (XRF)
  • Raman Spectroscopy
  • Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS)
  • Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM)
  • Liquid Density Measurements
