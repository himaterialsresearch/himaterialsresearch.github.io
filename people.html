Our Team

The Hawaii Institute of Materials Research (HIMaR) is a virtual interdisciplinary institute that brings together researchers from several colleges at the University of Hawaii, including the School of Ocean and Earth Science and Technology (SOEST), the College of Engineering, and the College of Natural Sciences. HIMaR participants collaborate across disciplines to advance research in sustainable materials, sensors, catalysis, and nanotechnology.
