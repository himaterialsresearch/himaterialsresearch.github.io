Welcome to HIMaR

The Hawaii Institute of Materials Research (HIMaR) is a collaborative research hub dedicated to advancing the science and engineering of materials for next-generation applications. Our mission is to support interdisciplinary innovation across materials chemistry, nanotechnology, sustainability, and advanced manufacturing.

Based at the University of Hawaii, HIMaR serves as a nexus for academic, industry, and federal partnerships aimed at accelerating discovery and workforce development in materials science.
